Mein Mutausbruch

I've written a book targeted towards women in their 30's who don't know yet what to do with their lives. Stuck in a society where people tend to tell women what to do, how to behave, how to dress and what to do with your body, it's not easy for women to figure out their own way. Having experienced the same I want to help those women with my book to find their very own ways.
